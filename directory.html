A Directory of Health Programmes

Many Rotarians are actively involved in programs aimed at enhancing healthcare. These initiatives include providing medical equipment, training, improving patient safety, and hands-on medical interventions such as cataract operations and cardiac surgery.

Rotary has extensive experience and expertise in engaging in sustainable healthcare programs. However, many clubs and Rotarians work in isolation without the opportunity to share lessons or learn from others. Our goal is to create an open directory of health programs to share experiences and lessons, building on our past successes.

Rotarians have shared information about the following programs:

  • A mental health program in Brazil
  • Screening for oral cancer in India
  • A diabetic clinic in Venezuela
  • A general clinic in Mexico
  • Music therapy in Idaho
  • Dental and eye care in Uganda
  • A cervical cancer screening program in India
  • A vision and dental clinic for the Bwindi Community

If you are involved in a health project please do share information your project. We will add your project to the list you can access below.
