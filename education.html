Educational Activities

There is so much we can learn from each other

Webinar

Many of our members are on the frontline facing challenges during this pandemic. We have been able to give them a voice and have a forum for them to share their views.

Our virtual round table discussions have been unique because the participants were heroes on the frontline – what they said came from the bottom of their hearts.

Our recent discussions:

  • End of life care in this pandemic led by Jeremy Johnson
  • Challenges in maternity care led by Himansu Basu
  • Covid testing facts and fiction led by Gabriele Mallapaty
  • Skin manifestation of Covid-19 – led by John Strasswimmer
  • Collaboration with Water and Sanitation Rotary Action Group – led by Ron Denham and John Philip
  • Medical equipment – procurement and donation – sharing good practices and learning lessons.
  • Covid-19 and medical ethics led by Dr Benjamin Stoff

This is a voluntary group – we use this to disseminate general information about new information of interest. Members can use the group to exchange ideas.
