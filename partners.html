Westport Rotary Club, CT USA Joseph Carpenter, MD jdcarp999@gmail.com We just completed a project at the Bwindi Community Hospital in Kanungu, Uganda. We outfitted 4 new ICU rooms and provided radiology equipment and general ward supplies. We worked with several other US Rotary clubs, the local Rotary club, ProjectCURE, Yale University, and the Catholic Medical Mission Board (NYC) for this project worth about a million US dollars.
